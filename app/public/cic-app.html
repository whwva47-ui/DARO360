Alpha.date Operator Guide
Three operating modes, hook rules, and scripts for every client scenario.
⚡ The Golden Hook Rule

Every message and letter must start with a strong hook in ALL CAPITAL LETTERS. 4–7 words. No punctuation at the end. Never repeat the same hook. This applies to ALL three categories.

📍 When to use Category 1

Use on the /chance page — winks, profile likes, views, first messages, and letters.

👋 He sent a wink
DJ, THAT WINK WAS JUST THE BEGINNING and I have a feeling you already know what comes next. What made you stop on my profile?
❤ He liked your profile
Robert, MOST PEOPLE SCROLL PAST WITHOUT STOPPING and I noticed you did not. What caught your attention?
📝 Letter (max 300 chars, one paragraph)
YOU HAVE A PRESENCE THAT STAYS WITH YOU and I mean that in the rarest way — the kind that lingers hours after you first noticed it. What has shaped you most?
💬 Category 2 Rules
ONE SENTENCE ONLY. 15–25 words maximum. No emojis.

Texting Factory Guide
Rules for chathomebase.com operators. Follow these to protect your account.
📏 Non-negotiable rules
75–250
Characters per reply — Under 75 is too short, over 250 gets cut off. Target 120–200 for best results.
✓
Always include a CTA — a question, an invitation, or something that makes him want to respond.
✕
Never suggest meeting in person. Redirect warmly if he asks.
✕
Never share contact info — no phone, WhatsApp, Instagram, email.
✕
No explicit content — flirty and suggestive only. Never crude.
✕
No emojis — Texting Factory flags emoji use.
✕
Never mention the platform name or that you are an operator.
